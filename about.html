Who We Are

DbData.in is a trusted B2B data intelligence company, built by a team of seasoned software engineers and data analysts with over 14 years of experience in data processing and secure data delivery. We specialize in providing structured, high-quality business databases that empower companies across India to connect, market, and grow with confidence.

Our Mission & Vision
Mission:

To make clean, structured, and actionable business data accessible to every organization, big or small.

Vision:

To become India's most reliable and comprehensive provider of verified B2B databases, industry directories, and business intelligence solutions.

Our Story

DbData.in was born from a deep understanding of the Indian business ecosystem and the need for dependable, scalable, and precise data. Our founders have worked with international clients on data-intensive projects, and recognized a common challenge: the lack of accessible, verified, and clean business data in India. We launched DbData.in to fill that gap—one dataset at a time.

What We Do

We provide meticulously formatted business data in Excel, CSV, and PDF formats. Our offerings include:

  • Pan-India databases across SMEs, MSMEs, corporates, and niche sectors.
  • Verified professional directories (HR, real estate, healthcare, education, etc.).
  • Telecalling/telemarketing-ready data.
  • Custom datasets for analytics, marketing, sales, or AI/ML training.

With data attributes like company name, address, contact info, services/products, and more, our datasets are ready for immediate action.

How We Work

We source data from public domains, business listings, government records, stock-exchange companies, and verified digital footprints. Every dataset undergoes:

  • AI-powered validation to ensure structure and accuracy
  • Manual verification for context, relevance, and compliance
  • LinkedIn verification for professional directories (especially HR)

We honor privacy and NEVER provide personal/individual data. All data is delivered securely via S3, SFTP, or email, in your preferred format (CSV, Excel, PDF).

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of data do you provide?

We offer structured B2B datasets across industries like real estate, healthcare, HR, education, manufacturing, and more. The data includes company name, contact info, address, industry, and other relevant fields—delivered in Excel, CSV, or PDF format.

How do you collect your data?

We gather data from public sources such as government portals, industry directories, stock exchange listings, and verified digital platforms. We use a mix of AI tools and manual validation to ensure accuracy.

Is the data verified or just scraped from the web?

Every dataset undergoes a two-step process: AI-powered structuring & validation Manual verification for accuracy, context, and duplicates Some directories (like HR contacts) are also LinkedIn-verified.

Can I request a custom dataset for my industry or region?

Absolutely. We offer customized datasets based on your industry, location, role, or other filters. Just contact us with your requirements.

Do you offer any API or automated integration?

Currently, we provide downloadable files only. However, we are working on API-based access and automation features in future plans.

What use cases can your data support?

Our data helps with: Lead generation & cold outreach Telecalling/telemarketing campaigns Market research & analysis Building AI/ML training datasets Competitor mapping Sales team targeting

Is this data GDPR or privacy-compliant?

Yes. We do not share personal or sensitive information. All our data is collected from publicly accessible sources and intended for B2B communication only.

How often is your data updated?

Our data is regularly updated depending on the category. Some high-demand datasets (like real estate and HR) are refreshed monthly or quarterly to maintain relevance and accuracy.

Can I see a sample before buying?

Yes! We provide free samples for most datasets so you can check the structure and quality before purchase.

What formats do you offer for data delivery?

You can choose between Excel (.xlsx), CSV, or PDF files. We also support delivery via secure email, S3 links, or SFTP.
